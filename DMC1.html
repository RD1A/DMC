Trish


Trish is a hauntingly beautiful woman who invites Dante to Mallet Island, gateway to the devil world. She possesss supernatural powers and vast knowledge about devils. It is said she knows Dante's secret history. But what is her true identity and purpose?



Nelo Angelo


Since many of the greater devils appearing in literature were once greater angels, it is not surprising that this black knight is called Nelo Angelo, the "black angel". It is said that he fights his enemy fairly and squarely, a rarity in a warrior from hell. If he is truly a practitioner of honor, why does he follow the Dark Power?



Mundus


The Prince of Darkness himself. After his defeat and sealing at the hands of Sparda, he seeks to return and reclaim a world he has longed to rule. With his four demon generals at his side he sets his plans into motion.



Sparda


The legendary dark knight. Taking pity on the humans, he weilded the sword that bore his name and pushed the demonic tide back to hell and sealed them off from the human world along with his own powers. Later, he married a human named Eva, who then bears twin sons. Sparda gave Yamato and Rebellion, his keepsake swords to his sons Vergil and Dante, respectively. He died later of a reason unknown.



Eva


The legendary dark knight. Mother of Dante and Vergil and wife of Sparda. She is a human and has been a strong motherly figure to the twins until her death at the hands of Mundus's demons.
